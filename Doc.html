What is Consciousness Hub?

Consciousness Hub is an online community where people explore reality beyond the surface level.

We're for people who:

  • Feel like there's more to life than what we're told
  • Want to understand consciousness, not just believe in it
  • Are curious about meditation, intuition, or OBEs
  • Need a space to ask questions without judgment

We bring together:

  • Science and experience (not one or the other)
  • Structure and freedom (clear paths, but you choose your own pace)
  • Beginners and advanced explorers (everyone belongs)

This isn't:

  • A religion (no belief required)
  • A course (you're not here to pass a test)
  • A guru-worship space (we question everything)

This is:

A community. A space. A home for people exploring consciousness seriously.
